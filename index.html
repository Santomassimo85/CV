Who am I?

My name is Luca Santomassimo and I'm years old. I was born in Naples, Italy, and I lived in Pompeii with my parents until I moved to Avellino, a beautiful city in italian appennines, where I currently live.

As a child I enjoyed spending my time playing with computers and video games, and I was always fascinated by the world of technology and the web. My first "playground" was the Commodore 64 console, where as a toddler I took great fun changing the loader screen color with a bit of "coding".

I kept feeding my curiosity for Tech when I embarked on my studies at an industrial technical institute where I chose electrical technology specialization.

Even if I worked in several fields, not alwasy concerning IT, in my free time I always kept studying some basic programming languages, also developing some websites, never letting down the dream of an IT career.

In 2024, I finally decide to try and change my life and become a professional in the field I am most passionate about, the world of Tech, by enrolling in the Epicode degree program.
